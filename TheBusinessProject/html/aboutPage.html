Our Founder

Martinease "Martin" Mortrinio

Brickz founder

Martin was captivated by the endless possibilities of LEGO bricks. He spent most of his life in information technologies in MIT until he retired from the field to pursue his dream.
He turned his passion into reality, founding Brickz to bring joy and creativity to builders of all ages. As a child, he would play with lego bricks and spent hours of his time working on grand lego castles and little lego machines. He credits his choice to enter IT to his primed problem solving brain. All thanks to lego. With each set meticulously designed to inspire, Martin invites you to join him on a journey where every click of a brick unlocks a world of imagination and endless possibilities.
